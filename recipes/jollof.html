Jollof Rice

Nigerian Jollof Rice

9ija jollof popularly known as party jollof is one Nigerian meal that cuts across every tribe and ethnic group in the country, and among the different ways it may be cooked and it's preparation methods and ingridents varies from individuals to ethnic groupsbut one thing is for sure everybody loves jollof! and a party without jollof it's certainly not a party for sure.

Ingreidents

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Tomato, both paste and fresh
  • Seasoning
  • Groundnut oil or Butter
  • pepper
  • Salt
  • Creyfish
  • spices
  • Meat, Chicken or Turkey
  • Vegetables

Jollof Preparation Steps

  1. Wash your pepper, fresh tomato and onion
  2. Blend them together
  3. Properly wash your rice or perboil it
  4. Heat the vegetable oil or butter over medium heat
  5. Chop or slice onion inside the oil and fry for a little while
  6. Add tomato past and cook for a while
  7. Add blended fresh tomato, pepper and onion and fry dry
  8. Add spices and water, allow to boil
  9. Add rice, salt and chicken and cook to done and serve
