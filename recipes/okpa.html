Okpa

Okpa (pronounced á»Œkpa) is a dish prepared by the Igbo people with a type of beans known as Bambara beans/nuts. It is common in Enugu state and some other eastern states of Nigeria, like Anambra and Imo state

Okpa is prepared in a very special way. It can be eaten alone or enjoy in fullest with Tea, Pap, juice, soft drinks etc. also it's neutritional benefits are among the things that make it special and very healthy.

Ingreidents

  • Bambara nuts(okpa) flour
  • Red oil or palm fruit
  • cray fish
  • Seasoning(optional)
  • Fish(optional)
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Vegetables(optional)
  • Okpa wrapper

Okpa Preparation Steps

  1. Sieve the okpa flour in a bowl
  2. Mix with red oil and mould with your hand until you achieve unity in colour
  3. Add cray fish, seasoning, salt and mix
  4. Boil water to lukewarm temperature, pour into the miture to your desired texture and mix properly
  5. Add blended pepper and stir
  6. Put enough water in a pot and boil. Ensure the water is enough to cover the wrapped okpa
  7. Wash the Wrapper and wrap the mixture to your desired size
  8. Put your fish inside the different wrapped okpa, if you have
  9. Put the wrapped okpa into the steaming hot water and boil for at least an hour and serve
